Mushrooms are a type of fungi that grow in a variety of habitats, from forests to fields to decomposing logs. They come in many different shapes, sizes, and colors, and are used for food, medicine, and other purposes. A mushroom is a fruitful body of fungus which is usually produced above the ground on soil or other nutrients. We only concentrate on mushrooms and do not consider fungus species.
These Mushrooms are majorly classified into 3 categories namely:

  • Boletus
  • Lactarius
  • Russula
